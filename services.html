Quick Set Concrete

Quick Set Concrete is used in making concrete repairs where rapid setting is required. Ideal for repairing broken edges of concrete steps and curbs.

Rapid Strength Concrete

Rapid Strength Concrete (RSC) is a type of concrete that gains strength quickly. Concrete strengths above 3,000 psi can be obtained in as little as 2 hours.

Learn more

Easy Flow Concrete

EasyFlow ready-mixed concrete dramatically improves workability and finish for all your concrete needs. EasyFlow is less sticky and flows more readily to provide greater all-round productivity.

Learn more

Self Compact Concrete

Self-Compacting Concrete (SCC) is a concrete that has an ability to flow under its own weight, to fill the required space or formwork completely and to produce a dense and adequately homogenous material without a need for vibrating compaction.

Learn more

Plastering Concrete

Plastering of concrete surface is the application of lean mortar to enhance the appearance of the surface. Different methods are used to plaster concrete surfaces such as dense concrete, low-weight concrete and mixed concrete surfaces.

Learn more

Fiber Re-infored Concrete

The fiber re-inforced concrete provides superior resistance to cracking in hardened state concrete, as well as maximum resistance to damage from heavy impact. Steel fibre reinforced concrete can be used for industrial floors, concrete structures and external hardstandings.
